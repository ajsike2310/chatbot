IEDC

 Catalyst- Mar Baselios IEDC

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre of Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology, Catalyst was inaugurated in the year 2013 with a purpose of inspiring students to become independent engineers by exposing then to the world of Entrepreneurship through Innovation. The Centre aims in sharpening the skills of students, broadening their knowledge base and equipping them with technical and non-technical qualities that an engineer need.

Rather than pushing students to startup, the centre believes in inculcating the entrepreneurial spirit in students. The members have identified the true joy of self-learning and they passionately involve in brining life into their ideas, to solve the problems that they see around. They know the importance of spreading this fire to ignite the minds of their fellow students and hence have dedicated all their work in IEDC for this purpose, besides working on their own individual projects. Catalyst Mar Baselios IEDC aims in helping the students follow their dreams and passion to help them depart MBCET campus not just as engineering graduates, but as real engineers who seek and solve problems.

NATURE Club

NATURE CLUB

NATURE CLUB (PRAKRUTHI)

“The Greatest Threat to Nature is to believe that someone else will save it.”

Realizing this, “Prakruthi”, the Nature club of Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology has always been committed in sensitizing the students on issues related to Environment. The main objective of the nature club is to create awareness among students on the need for environmental protection, conservation of our resources and to make our campus green and eco-friendly.

Nature Clubs bring students closer to nature and make them realize the importance of maintaining an eco-friendly environment. The Nature Club believes that nature teaches life’s lessons in its own subtle ways and enables our youth to share their responsibilities in preserving a healthy environment and to take steps to avoid environmental degradation in their individual capacity as well as in a group. We empower and sensitise our students on issues related to environmental degradation and take up meaningful environmental activities and projects.

Faculty Co-ordinator

Dr. Jaya S Pillai, Assistant Professor, Department of Civil Engineering

Student Co-ordinator

Mr. Alan A. Vincent, S6 CE1

World Water Day Celebration

As part of the World Water Day Celebrations, the Nature Club of MBCET – ‘Prakruthi’ conducted a quiz competition on March 22, 2024 for students based on the global campaign theme of this year ‘WATER FOR PEACE’.

World Environment Day celebrations

As part of the World Environment Day (June 5)celebrations, Nature club (Prakruthi) organised distribution of 100 saplings to MBCET staff members on 6th June 2024. Dr.Jaya S.Pillai and Ms. Akhila A.M. were the  coordinators of the program.

Public Speaking Club

The Oratory club was formed to focus on and bring up public speaking activities in our college. Central to the club’s activities is its participation in various competitions, including Model United Nations (MUN) conferences held in different colleges. MUN conferences provide a unique platform for students to engage in diplomatic simulations, representing different countries and debating pressing global issues. Performing Arts Club

PERFORMING ARTS CLUB MBCET

The Performing Arts Club at MBCET was established in 2017 with a clear vision of fostering creativity and providing a platform for students to engage in diverse forms of performing arts. Since its inception, the club has remained committed to promoting artistic expression and providing students with opportunities to showcase their talents. The club regularly organizes a variety of events, offering students a space to express their passion for performing arts. In addition to performances, the club organizes workshops on different artistic disciplines, allowing students to nurture their skills and explore new forms of art. These workshops help cultivate a deeper understanding of performance techniques and broaden the creative horizons of participants. Meetings are held periodically to evaluate past activities, discuss future events, and ensure that the club continues to grow in line with its objectives. The club’s efforts extend beyond academics, as it aims to engage students in activities that keep their minds fresh and invigorated.

The Performing Arts Club also serves as a forum for students to discuss the evolution of art, exchange ideas, and discover new talents within the MBCET community. Through these initiatives, the club plays an essential role in promoting the growth of artistic expression and creativity among students.

Faculty In-charge: Ms. Akhila A M, Assistant Professor, Civil Engineering Department

Club Secretary: Basil Anil, EEE Department

By supporting the artistic development of students and promoting a culture of creativity, the Performing Arts Club continues to be a vibrant and integral part of campus life at MBCET

FOSS Club

         Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) are those software that are liberally licensed to grant users the freedom to run the program for any purpose, to study and modify the program, and to redistribute copies of either the original or modified program (without having to pay royalties to previous developers). The main objective of the club is to adopt and promote the Free and Open Source Software. It can include both the system software as well as the application software. System Software include- GNU/Linux, Apache Web Server, BSD etc. and Application Software include OpenOffice, Gimp, TeX and LaTeX typesetting systems etc.

YOGA Club

YOGA CLUB

“Yoga is the journey of the self through the self to the self”.

Yoga has been gaining immense popularity due to the short-term as well as long-term benefits that it provides. The aims of the yoga enthusiasts are extremely varied. Some are particularly inspired by the spiritual element that yoga provides; others by the increased fitness and flexibility that it results in. Some people find solutions to suffering from varied health disorders and there are others who achieve an all-round development of a calm-stress free mind and a fit body.

Our college proudly supports yoga through club activities. Hours a day sitting at desk or more hours doing homework cause a young body to tense up. Social and family pressures and unfortunately, anxiety, abuse and bullying also take their toll. Stress is a major obstacle to academic achievement, and yoga’s stress relief powers have been shown to boost student performance.

Yoga and Meditation club in our college has its activities going on actively. Club conducts the activities once in a week. The activity includes theoretical, practical classes and convey knowledge about importance of yoga and meditation under the guidance of trained teachers who has about 10 years of experience. While we may speak different languages and come from different states, our problems and questions about life are similar. Modern science has made great strides in helping us improve our standard of living, it has lacked in a holistic approach to help people overcome pain in life and gain lasting happiness. Each of us are on a spiritual journey with life lessons to learn. For those of us who are conscious of spiritual journey and want to progress spiritually, yoga and meditation club would like to help by providing you with a road map so that you can make the best use of every precious moment of your life.

Yoga and Meditation club activity have been conducted weekly one hour. Which includes Meditations, Pranayama- a process of directing energy in our body, Yoga postures and Sooryanamaskara. Yoga is not a religion; it is a way of living that aims towards a healthy mind in a healthy body.

µLearn

μLearn is a pioneering peer-driven learning platform that fosters skill development through a unique microlearning approach. Designed to meet the evolving needs of learners, μLearn breaks down industry-relevant skills into actionable tasks, enabling users to progress in a structured and gamified environment. Learners earn Karma Points for each completed task, with progress tracked on dynamic leaderboards, fostering healthy competition and engagement.
A defining feature of μLearn is its emphasis on community collaboration. Hosted on Discord, the platform brings together proactive learners, industry leaders, and mentors, creating a vibrant ecosystem for knowledge exchange. Tasks created within μLearn are not only crafted to meet real-world standards but are also vetted for quality and relevance by industry experts and mentors. Proof of work submissions undergo thorough validation, ensuring that the learning outcomes are impactful and credible. By combining microlearning, gamification, and community-driven engagement, μLearn catalyzes developing industry-aligned skills while promoting inclusivity, collaboration, and innovation.

Block Hub

Introduction

The world is changing, and so should we. Blockchain technology and Web 3.0 have the potential to revolutionize industries, disrupt traditional industries, and empower individuals. Web 3.0 is creating more jobs and opportunities than ever before. Considering this, we propose the creation of a club in our college in collaboration with Block Hub, which will be called Block Hub MBCET. Here, we will be breaking down the complex world of blockchain to bring students the essence of Web 3.0.

Vision

To lead the charge in understanding, embracing, and shaping the future of the internet in MBCET. Our vision is to empower a community of forward thinkers who explore the limitless possibilities of Web 3.0, fostering innovations and decentralized solutions.

Mission

Our mission is to educate, inspire, and connect individuals in exploring Web 3.0 technologies. We aim to foster innovation, advocate for decentralization, and empower our members to shape a more inclusive, secure, and decentralized digital future.

Objectives

  • Education: Provide resources, workshops, and events to educate members and the public about blockchain technology and Web 3.0 applications.
  • Innovation: Foster collaboration among developers, researchers, and entrepreneurs to drive innovation in the blockchain space.
  • Inclusivity: Create a diverse and inclusive community that welcomes individuals from all backgrounds.
