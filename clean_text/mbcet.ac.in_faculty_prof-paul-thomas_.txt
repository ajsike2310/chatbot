Dr. Paul Thomas
Dr. Paul Thomas completed his B.Tech,  M Tech and Ph.D from CET Trivandrum. He was the Professor-in-charge of M Tech programs at Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirappally. He has over 41 years of experience in Research, Industry and Teaching. He worked as a Scientist in ISRO, and held positions as Manager, Senior Manager and Delivery Head in IT industry. He has chaired sessions in several National and International conferences.
