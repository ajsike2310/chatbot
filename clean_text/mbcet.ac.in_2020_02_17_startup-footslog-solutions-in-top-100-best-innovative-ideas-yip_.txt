Team Footslog Solutions has been adjudged as one among the 102 Best Innovative Ideas for Young Innovators Program (YIP) 2019-2022. The idea name is “Rehabilitation and Mobility in One”. A special window of mentoring, scholarships, grants and specialised nurturing has been opened to them.
Footslog Solutions is a Startup incubated in Catalyst – Mar Baselios IEDC. The team of Footslog Solutions comprises of Nigel Thomas, Nithin Raju, Jeevan Jagesh, Rohit Paul of the 2015-2019 batch of Mechanical Engineering.
