Team EcoRich Technologies bagged the position of Second Runner-up in the She Power Hackathon organized by Kerala Startup Mission in association with the US State Department and world learning as implementing partners.
She Power is a virtual program comprising a Hackathon and a three-day summit to empower women tech startup founders and startups in India. The Hackathon aims to find woman-led technology solutions to solve critical issues faced by women in India.
