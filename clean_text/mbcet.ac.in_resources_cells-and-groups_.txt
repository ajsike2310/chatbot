The Institute has a Environment Management Cell that facilitates the smooth functioning of the three cells namely Waste Management cell, water management cell, Energy management cell  under the umbrella of

P. He gave deep insights on solid waste segregation, converting plastic waste to useful plastic, earning from waste and agencies for waste collection. WATER MANAGEMENT CELL
Effective water management is essential for sustainable development and the preservation of natural resources. The
Water Management Cell
of our institution is dedicated to implementing and promoting efficient water usage practices, ensuring resource conservation, and fostering environmental responsibility.
Water Management Cell, MBCET was constituted with the following objectives to achieve.
OBJECTIVES:
To ensure proper maintenance of the water bodies and water distribution system of the Campus
To monitor the availability of adequate, good quality water in the Campus
To assist in the process of adopting Scientific Strategies for Water Conservation in the Campus.
To spread awareness about Water Conservation and Management to all students,faculty and staff members of the Campus by placing display boards, conducting seminars, competitions etc.
Key Initiatives
Rainwater Harvesting
: Installation of systems to capture and utilize rainwater, reducing dependency on external water sources.
Water Recycling
: Establishment of wastewater treatment plants for recycling and reusing water within the campus.
Leakage Detection and Maintenance
: Regular audits to identify and repair leaks promptly, minimizing water wastage.
Efficient Irrigation
: Adoption of drip and sprinkler systems to optimize water usage in landscaping and green spaces.
Awareness Campaigns
: Hosting workshops, seminars, and awareness drives on water conservation for students and staff.
The Water Management Cell ensures the responsible use of water resources, contributes to environmental conservation, and sets an example for sustainable practices. These efforts align with global sustainability goals and help our institution maintain a balance between growth and ecological stewardship.
We are committed to fostering a culture of responsibility and accountability in water management, ensuring a greener future for generations to come.

Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA)
Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA) is a flagship programme of Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Govt. of India. It is a scheme for selected Higher educational Institutions (HEIs) of the country where they adopt villages for their development. Faculty and students are involved in village development plan in collaboration with district administration.

Anti-Narcotic Cell
Anti-Narcotic Cell is committed to creating and maintaining a campus environment that is free of alcohol and substance abuse. It is an active body comprising of teachers and student representatives aiming at making the institution a peaceful and progressive campus, free of any abuse of alcohol or other narcotic substance
The activities of anti-narcotic cell include organizing awareness programmes with the aid of government organizations, arranging campaigns, conducting seminars etc. The cell also monitors the entire campus to make sure its mission is executed in the right way.
We keep close contact with the excise and police departments and arrange programs to make the students aware of the strong legal force working against narcotics. The club supports the proactive interventions by Government, NGOs, De-addiction centres etc. for a drug free world.
