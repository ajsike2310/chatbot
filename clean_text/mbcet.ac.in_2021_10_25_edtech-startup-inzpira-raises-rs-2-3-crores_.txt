Edtech Startup Inzpira raised 310K USD (Rupees 2.3 Crores) in seed round. Angel investors Raj Nair, Chairman, Avalon Consulting and Gopi Latpate, Executive Director of JLL led the seed round.
Inzpira is India’s only live online 1:1 platform for Language Learning & Test Preparation that uses AI enabled platform and lets users instantly connect with expert trainers and learn anytime from anywhere.
Sruthy Ramesh is the Co-founder & COO at Inzpira. She is an alumnus of 2010-2014 batch of CSE.
