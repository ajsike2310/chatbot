ACM
The Association for Computing Machinery is an international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947, and is the world’s largest scientific and educational computing society. The ACM is an umbrella organization for academic and scholarly interests in computer science. Its motto is “Advancing Computing as a Science & Profession”.
