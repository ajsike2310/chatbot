Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology (MBCET) welcomes its first International Research Scholar, Mr. Zayar Sit Paing Thu from Myanmar, under the ASEAN-INDIA Research Training Fellowship (AIRTF) of the Department of Science and Technology (DST). Mr. Zayar Sit Paing Thu will do research under the guidance of Mr. Bobin Saji George, Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, MBCET in the field of Nuclear Technology.
