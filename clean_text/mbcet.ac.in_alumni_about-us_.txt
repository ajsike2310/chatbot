About us
MBCET Alumni Association – A Legacy That Transcends Borders
At
Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology (MBCET)
, education is more than academic excellence—it’s about building lifelong bonds, nurturing dreams, and empowering leaders. The
MBCET Alumni Association
stands as a vibrant testament to this legacy, uniting thousands of graduates from every corner of the globe into a single, powerful community.
Whether you once walked through the lush green pathways of Nalanchira or sat in those iconic lecture halls that sparked your ambition, you’re part of something bigger. MBCET alumni are not just engineers—they’re
visionaries, entrepreneurs, researchers, educators, and global citizens
who are making a mark in the world.
A Global Network Like No Other
With a proud presence in over
30 countries
, the MBCET Alumni Association is more than a network—it’s a
global movement
of shared purpose and lifelong connection. Our 37 regional and professional subgroups are buzzing with collaboration, mentorship, and camaraderie.
Our alumni community thrives across:
The innovation capitals of
North America
The cultural hubs of

The fast-growing tech corridors of
Asia

Singapore, Japan, South Korea
, and

The economic powerhouses of the
Middle East
, such as
Qatar, UAE, Oman, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, and Bahrain
And dynamic destinations like

Ireland
From
boardrooms in Toronto
to

What We Do
The MBCET Alumni Association:
Connects
graduates across batches, branches, and geographies
Supports
career development through job referrals, mentoring, and networking
Celebrates
the success stories of alumni around the world
Collaborates
with the institution to guide and inspire current students
Contributes
to community and campus development initiatives
Our events, webinars, regional meetups, professional panels, and outreach programs are designed to keep the bond alive—because at MBCET, the journey doesn’t end with graduation.
