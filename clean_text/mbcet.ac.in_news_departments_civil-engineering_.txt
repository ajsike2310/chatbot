MBCET has been accredited by
NAAC with grade ‘A’ (CGPA: 3.13).
We are the only Engineering college in Kerala with NAAC ‘A’ grade and NBA accreditation for all B.Tech programmes.
The NAAC Peer Team visited the MBCET campus on 26, 27 and 28 September 2016 to assess the performance of the institution. The team was headed by
Padmashri Prof. Rajpal S Sirohi
, Former Director, IIT Delhi and Bharat Ratna Lokapriya Gopinath Bordoloi Chair Professor, Tezpur University (Chairman);
Dr. Amiyakumar Rath
, Professor of Computer Science, Veer Surendra Sai University, Odisha (Member, Co-ordinator) and
Dr. Vlias Karjinni
, Principal, Kolhapur Institute of Technology’s College of Engineering, Maharashtra (Member).
