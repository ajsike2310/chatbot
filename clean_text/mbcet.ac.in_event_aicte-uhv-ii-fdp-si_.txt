Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology (Autonomous), Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala is conducting AICTE sponsored 5-Day Faculty Development Programme, Student Induction (FDP-SI) on Universal Human Values-II from 29-08-2022 to 2-09-2022. The FDP is completely residential and free accommodation (AICTE sponsored) will be provided to the participants.
Minimum Eligibility for participation:
• One 3-day FDP-SI (UHV)
• One 8-day UHV FDP
