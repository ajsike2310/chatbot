NASE ‘19 is a national level technical symposium, conducted by Department of Civil Engineering, MBCET, Thiruvananthapuram, which aims at shedding light upon the recent technological achievements in the field of Structural Engineering and Geotechnical Engineering. The national conference aspires to provide an arena for academicians, professionals and research scholars to explore and share innovative insights on various aspects of Nano materials in concrete, Special concrete, Soil structure interaction, Quality checks in concrete and fracture mechanics.
