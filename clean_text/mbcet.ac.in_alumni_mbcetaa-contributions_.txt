Contributions of the MBCET Alumni Association (MBCETAA)
The
Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology Alumni Association (MBCETAA)
continues to play a vital role in enriching the academic and cultural ecosystem of the institution. Driven by a spirit of gratitude and commitment to give back, MBCETAA has made substantial contributions during the academic year, reinforcing its support for student development, academic excellence, and institutional growth.
Key contributions made by MBCETAA include:
Flagship Sponsorship of Crossroads
MBCETAA serves as the
principal financial sponsor
of the college’s
prestigious techno-cultural fest, Crossroads
, contributing
₹1,00,000 annually
. This support significantly enhances the scale and quality of the event, encouraging student innovation, leadership, and cultural expression.
Support for Department-Level Technical Fests
To promote technical excellence and student engagement across disciplines, MBCETAA provides
₹1,00,000 annually (₹20,000 per department)
towards the organization of
department-level technical festivals
, fostering experiential learning and inter-institutional collaboration.
MBCETAA Outstanding Performer Award
Instituted to recognize holistic student development, this prestigious award is conferred upon the
Best Outgoing Student
of the previous academic year. The selection is based on an integrated assessment of academic performance, co-curricular achievements, and extra-curricular involvement.
MBCETAA Best Project Award
To encourage innovation and real-world problem-solving among final year students, the Alumni Association presents (during Exodos, graduation ceremony every year) the
Best Project Award
in each department. The award includes a
cash prize of ₹10,000
along with
certificates for all group members
, celebrating exemplary project work and technical creativity.
Support for International Conferences
In support of the college’s academic and research endeavours, MBCETAA contributed
₹25,000
towards the
organization of International Conferences
, promoting global academic exchange and scholarly dissemination.
Support for Social Causes and Disaster Relief
Reflecting its strong commitment to social responsibility, MBCETAA has consistently responded to humanitarian needs by contributing to various
relief and rehabilitation efforts
. The association has extended
financial support for initiatives such as the Kerala Flood Relief Fund and Wayanad Relief Fund (Rs 1,00,000)
, demonstrating solidarity with communities in distress and reinforcing the college’s ethos of compassion and civic engagement.
Through these initiatives, MBCETAA continues to bridge the gap between the past and present, fostering a legacy of excellence, mentorship, and institutional pride. Its contributions not only support student and faculty initiatives but also reflect the enduring bond between the alumni and their alma mater.
