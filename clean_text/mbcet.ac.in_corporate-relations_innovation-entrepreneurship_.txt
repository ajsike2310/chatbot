Innovation & Entrepreneurship
The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) supports the development of entrepreneurship through skill training, strategies and mentoring. If you’re trying to refine an idea, the centre will help you get into the entrepreneurial mindset, generating the inspiration and creativity you need to think outside the box. The incubation centre help with things like the process of launching your startup, business modeling and planning, managing human resources and financing strategy.
