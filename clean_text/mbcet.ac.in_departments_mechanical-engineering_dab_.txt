DAB
“The Departmental Advisory Board (DAB) serves as a critical mechanism for ensuring curriculum relevance and alignment with evolving industry demands. Composed of esteemed representatives from prominent academic institutions, diverse industry sectors, successful alumni, and departmental faculty, the DAB provides a multifaceted perspective on programme development. Its primary functions include the formulation and revision of programme goals and objectives, as well as the systematic review and analysis of the curriculum to identify and address any discrepancies with current industry requirements. Through its expert feedback and recommendations, the DAB facilitates continuous curriculum enhancement, ensuring the programme remains academically rigorous and directly responsive to the needs of the professional field.”
