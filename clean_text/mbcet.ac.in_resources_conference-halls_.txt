Conference Halls
Our Campus is provided with well-furnished halls for conducting Conferences & Seminars. These halls are provided with permanent audio, video and projection system. Scholars in various fields regularly give Talks and Lectures on various latest topics to our students. Thus our students are exposed to the recent technologies and trends. Faculty Development Programs are conducted in the Seminar Halls to update the knowledge of our faculty members.
